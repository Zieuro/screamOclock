Privacy Policy for Scream O'Clock

Effective Date: September 08, 2025

Scream O'Clock is designed for scare actor schedule rotations and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking

How the App Works

Scream O'Clock simply displays rotation schedules and provides countdown notifications. All scheduling data stays on the device and is not transmitted to us.
